Conclusions¶

At the end, the target to focus on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. It seems most of not shown patients don't have a chronic disease and does not have a Scholarship as shown in the last bar chart which shows that most of not shown up patients don't have Scholarship,Hipertension,Diabetes,Alcoholism and Handcap. However, There is some limitations in this dataset that I can not provide more detials example that location of the hospitals 'Neighbourhood' is limited as the highest data provided for a hospital is 4192 from around min 62299 so I can not exactly determine for each location of the hospital if a patient will show up or not.
